Lasagna

Description

Makign lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find the ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
